# Pricing Strategy
**Module Pricing Framework for PopSystem Platform**

## Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive pricing strategy for PopSystem's modular architecture, designed to maximize revenue while providing flexible, value-based pricing that scales with customer needs.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Value-Based Pricing**: Price reflects business value, not just features
2. **Transparent & Predictable**: No hidden fees or surprise charges
3. **Flexible Consumption**: Multiple models to match usage patterns
4. **Growth-Friendly**: Pricing that scales with customer success
5. **Competitive**: Market-informed pricing with clear differentiation

---

## Pricing Models Overview

| Model | When to Use | Best For | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|----------|
| Flat Module Fee | Predictable usage, fixed costs | Small-medium businesses | $49-499/mo per module |
| Usage-Based | Variable consumption | High-volume users | Per API call, per GB |
| Tiered | Different feature sets | Segment-based needs | Basic/Pro/Enterprise |
| Bundle Discounts | Multi-module adoption | Growing accounts | 3+ modules = 20% off |
| Enterprise Custom | Unique requirements | Large organizations | Custom negotiated |

## Enterprise Custom Pricing

### Factors Affecting Enterprise Pricing
1. **Volume/Scale**
   - Number of locations
   - Number of users
   - Transaction volume
   - Data volume

2. **Customization**
   - Custom integrations
   - Dedicated infrastructure
   - Custom features
   - White-label requirements

3. **Support Level**
   - Dedicated account manager
   - 24/7 support
   - Custom SLAs
   - Professional services

4. **Contract Terms**
   - Contract length (1-5 years)
   - Payment terms (annual, quarterly)
   - Committed growth
   - Renewal terms

### Enterprise Pricing Framework

#### Base Calculation
```
Enterprise Price =
  (Base Platform Fee × Location Multiplier) +
  (Module Fees × Volume Discount) +
  (Support Fee × SLA Level) +
  (Professional Services) +
  (Custom Development)
```

#### Example: 100-Location Franchise
```
Core Platform (Enterprise):          $999/mo
  × Location Multiplier (0.5):       × 50 locations = $49,950/mo

DAM (Enterprise):                    $499/mo
  - Volume Discount (40%):           $299/mo

Designer (Agency):                   $499/mo
  - Volume Discount (40%):           $299/mo

Workflow (Enterprise):               $599/mo
  - Volume Discount (40%):           $359/mo

MIS Pro (Complete):                  $1,999/mo
  - Volume Discount (40%):           $1,199/mo

White-Label (Complete):              $499/mo

Dedicated Account Manager:           $2,000/mo
Professional Services (10hrs/mo):    $1,500/mo

TOTAL:                               $56,605/mo
Annual Contract (15% discount):      $48,115/mo
3-Year Contract (25% discount):      $42,454/mo
```

### Volume Tiers for Locations

| Locations | Multiplier | Effective Cost per Location |
|-----------|------------|----------------------------|
| 1-10 | 1.0 | Full price |
| 11-50 | 0.8 | 20% reduction |

## Freemium & Trial Strategy

### Free Tier (Not Recommended)
**Decision**: No free tier to maintain product value and reduce support burden

### Trial Offerings

#### 14-Day Free Trial
- **Included**: All modules available
- **Limits**: Reduced limits (e.g., 10 locations, 5 GB storage)
- **No Credit Card**: Required only to activate after trial
- **Convert Rate Target**: 25%

#### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
- Available on annual plans
- Full refund if cancelled within 30 days
- Reduces risk for larger commitments

#### Pilot Programs (Enterprise)
- 90-day pilot at 50% discount
- Limited scope (one department/location)
- Converts to full contract or refund

## Pricing FAQs

**Q: What happens if I exceed my usage limits?**
A: You'll receive notifications at 80% and 90%. At 100%, you can either upgrade or purchase overage credits.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes for monthly plans. Annual plans have early termination fees (remaining months × 50%).

**Q: Do you offer non-profit discounts?**
A: Yes, 20% discount for registered 501(c)(3) organizations.

**Q: What's included in the Enterprise tier?**
A: Custom pricing based on your needs. Contact sales for a personalized quote.

**Q: Can I try before I buy?**
A: Yes, 14-day free trial with full access to all modules.

**Q: How does pricing work for seasonal businesses?**
A: Consider usage-based pricing or pause your subscription during off-season (data preserved).
