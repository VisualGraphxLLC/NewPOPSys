# Content Strategy - PopSystem

## Executive Summary

This content strategy defines PopSystem's approach to creating, distributing, and optimizing content across the buyer's journey. The strategy focuses on establishing thought leadership, educating the market, generating qualified leads, and nurturing prospects through data-driven, SEO-optimized content that addresses specific pain points and use cases.

## Content Vision & Objectives

### Vision
Position PopSystem as the trusted authority on modern population health management, value-based care transformation, and health equity through educational, actionable, and evidence-based content.

### Strategic Objectives

**Brand Awareness**
- Establish PopSystem as thought leader in population health technology
- Generate 15,000+ monthly organic website visitors by month 12
- Achieve top 3 search rankings for 20+ target keywords
- Build email list of 10,000+ qualified healthcare professionals

**Lead Generation**
- Generate 500+ marketing qualified leads (MQLs) in year 1
- Achieve 5% conversion rate from visitor to lead
- Support 30% of sales pipeline with content-driven leads
- Reduce cost per lead by 40% vs. paid advertising alone

**Sales Enablement**
- Provide sales team with content for every stage of buyer journey
- Achieve 70%+ content utilization by sales team
- Reduce sales cycle by 15% through educated prospects
- Support 5+ customer success stories per quarter

**Customer Education & Retention**
- Drive product adoption through educational content
- Reduce support tickets by 25% with self-service content
- Increase customer expansion by highlighting advanced features
- Achieve 90%+ customer retention through value demonstration

## SEO Strategy

### Technical SEO Foundation

**Website Infrastructure**:
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast page load times (<3 seconds)
- Clean URL structure
- XML sitemap and robots.txt
- Schema markup for rich snippets
- SSL certificate (HTTPS)
- Internal linking strategy

**On-Page Optimization**:
- Keyword-optimized title tags and meta descriptions
- Header tag hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Image alt text and optimization
- Keyword density and placement
- Content length and quality
- User engagement signals

### Keyword Strategy

**Primary Keywords** (High volume, high competition):
- Population health management
- Value-based care
- Care management software

- Care coordination software

- Social determinants of health

**Secondary Keywords** (Medium volume, medium competition):
- Population health analytics

**Long-Tail Keywords** (Lower volume, lower competition, higher intent):

- "FHIR-based care coordination software"
- "Population health software for FQHCs"
- "Cloud-based population health management system"
- "Population health platform pricing"
- "Epic alternative population health"

**Local SEO** (For regional targeting):
- "Population health software [city/state]"
- Healthcare IT companies [location]

### Content SEO Optimization

**Blog Post Formula**:
1. Target one primary keyword + 2-3 secondary keywords
2. Include primary keyword in title, first paragraph, conclusion
3. Use header tags with keyword variations
4. 1,200-1,800 word count for depth
5. Internal links to related content (3-5)
6. External links to authoritative sources (2-3)
7. Include relevant images with alt text
8. Add structured data markup
9. Optimize meta title and description
10. Include clear call-to-action

**Content Cluster Strategy**:
- Pillar page: Comprehensive guide (3,000+ words)
  - Example: "The Complete Guide to Population Health Management"
- Cluster content: 10-15 supporting blog posts
  - Example: "Care Gap Identification Strategies," "Risk Stratification Best Practices"
- Internal linking between pillar and cluster content

**Keyword Tracking**:
- Monthly ranking reports for target keywords
- Organic traffic and conversion tracking
- Competitor keyword analysis
- Identify new keyword opportunities

## Content Governance

### Quality Standards

**Voice & Tone**:
- Professional yet approachable
- Educational and helpful
- Evidence-based and credible
- Empathetic to customer challenges
- Conversational, not corporate

**Brand Guidelines**:
- Consistent terminology and messaging
- Approved logo and color usage
- Image style and quality standards
- Legal and compliance review process

**Editorial Standards**:
- Grammar and style guide (AP or Chicago)
- Fact-checking and accuracy
- Proper citations and attribution
- Readability targets (Flesch-Kincaid)
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)

### Review & Approval Process

**Content Creation Workflow**:
1. Ideation and planning (Content Manager)
2. Research and outlining (Writer)
3. First draft (Writer)
4. Peer review (Writer 2)
5. Subject matter expert review (if technical)
6. Editing (Content Manager)
7. Design/formatting (Multimedia Producer)
8. Legal/compliance review (if needed)
9. Final approval (Content Manager)
10. Publication and promotion

**Review SLAs**:
- Peer review: 2 business days
- SME review: 3 business days
- Legal review: 5 business days
- Total creation to publish: 2-3 weeks (standard), 1 week (rush)

### Content Maintenance

**Regular Updates**:
- Quarterly review of top-performing content
- Annual content audit of all published content
- Update statistics, examples, and links
- Refresh outdated information
- Redirect or delete low-performing content

**Content Lifecycle**:
- Evergreen content: Update annually
- Timely content: Archive after 6-12 months
- Product content: Update with releases
- Case studies: Refresh every 2 years
