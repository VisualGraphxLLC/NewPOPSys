## 1. Marketplace Overview

### 1.1 Platform Purpose
- **Service**: Connection platform between PopSystem customers and qualified service providers
- **Marketplace Types**:
  - **Installation Services**: Professional installation of print systems, signage, and displays
  - **Design Services**: Graphic design, layout, and pre-press services
  - **Consultation**: System optimization, workflow design, and training
- **Service Model**: Independent contractor marketplace (not employment)
- **Geographic Scope**: United States initially, expanding to other regions
- **Platform Role**: Facilitator only, not service provider

### 1.2 Marketplace Launch Timeline
- **v4 Release**: Initial marketplace launch with installation services
- **v5 Enhancements**: Designer marketplace and expanded categories
- **Future Phases**: Training, maintenance, and specialized services

### 1.3 Relationship Definition
- PopSystem is a platform operator, not an employer or principal
- Service providers are independent contractors
- Direct relationship between customer and service provider
- PopSystem facilitates connections but does not guarantee outcomes
- No employee-employer relationship created

## 2. Service Provider Registration and Requirements

### 2.1 Eligibility Requirements

#### 2.1.1 Basic Qualifications
- **Age**: 18+ years or legal business entity

- **Tax Status**: Valid taxpayer ID (EIN or SSN)

#### 2.1.2 Insurance Requirements
Service providers must maintain:
- **General Liability**: Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence
- **Professional Liability**: Minimum $500,000 (for design services)

- **Renewal**: Annual verification before certificate expiration

## 3. Bidding and Project Management

### 3.1 Bidding Process

#### 3.1.1 Project Posting
- Customer posts project with specifications
- Required details: scope, location, timeline, budget range
- Photos and technical documents attached
- Project visibility: All qualified providers in service area
- Posting duration: 48-72 hours typically

#### 3.1.2 Bid Submission
- **Response Window**: 24-72 hours from posting
- **Bid Components**:
  - Total price (itemized breakdown)
  - Timeline/schedule
  - Approach and methodology
  - Materials included/excluded
  - Warranty offered
  - Terms and conditions
  - Portfolio examples of similar work
- **Bid Limits**: Maximum 10 active bids per provider
- **Modification**: Bids editable until customer reviews
- **Binding Period**: Bids valid for 7 days minimum

#### 3.1.3 Customer Selection
- Customer reviews bids and provider profiles
- Direct messaging for questions/clarification
- Customer selects provider and accepts bid
- Contract formed between customer and provider
- PopSystem notified of selection

#### 3.1.4 Prohibited Bidding Practices
- Bid manipulation or collusion
- Off-platform solicitation to avoid fees
- False or misleading bid information
- Discriminatory pricing or practices
- Bid rigging or market allocation
- Retaliation against customers who don't select bid

### 3.2 Project Execution

#### 3.2.1 Communication
- All pre-contract communication through platform (recommended)
- Direct communication permitted after selection
- Platform messaging for dispute record
- Required updates: Material delays, scope changes, completion

#### 3.2.2 Scope Changes
- Written change orders required for scope modifications
- Customer approval required before additional work
- Pricing adjustments documented in platform
- Timeline impacts communicated promptly

#### 3.2.3 Project Completion
- Provider marks project complete in platform
- Customer reviews and approves completion
- Dispute period: 7 days for issues
- Final payment released after approval

## 4. Payment Terms

### 4.1 Payment Structure

#### 4.1.1 Platform Fee
- **Service Fee**: 8-15% of project value (tier-based)
  - **Starter Tier**: 15% (annual earnings < $25,000)
  - **Professional Tier**: 12% (annual earnings $25,000-$100,000)
  - **Elite Tier**: 8% (annual earnings > $100,000)
- **Charged To**: Service provider (deducted from customer payment)
- **Applies To**: Total project value including materials
- **Calculation**: Based on final agreed price (including change orders)

#### 4.1.2 Payment Processing
- **Customer Payment**: Due per agreement (deposit + milestones + final)
- **Platform Hold**: PopSystem holds customer payment in escrow
- **Provider Payout**: Released upon customer approval or dispute resolution
- **Processing Time**: 3-5 business days after release approval
- **Payment Method**: ACH direct deposit (preferred) or check
- **Minimum Payout**: $100 (smaller amounts rolled to next payout)

#### 4.1.3 Standard Payment Schedule
- **Deposit**: 25-50% upfront (for projects > $1,000)
- **Milestones**: Defined per project
- **Final Payment**: Upon completion and approval
- **Rush Fee**: Customer may offer expedite premium

### 4.2 Refunds and Disputes

#### 4.2.1 Customer Refund Rights
- Full refund if provider cancels before start
- Partial refund for incomplete work (prorated)
- Refund for material defects (corrective work failed)
- Dispute resolution determination
- No refund for customer convenience cancellation after start

#### 4.2.2 Provider Payment Protection
- Payment for completed work (per milestone)
- Protection against unfair customer cancellation
- Compensation for customer-caused delays (if agreed)
- Materials cost recovery (if customer cancels mid-project)

#### 4.2.3 Dispute Process
- Either party initiates dispute within 7 days of completion
- Parties negotiate resolution (15-day period)
- PopSystem mediation available (optional)
- Evidence submission: Photos, messages, documents
- Binding arbitration if unresolved (per main Terms of Service)

## 7. Dispute Resolution

### 7.1 Internal Dispute Process

#### 7.1.1 Dispute Categories
- **Quality Disputes**: Work not meeting specifications
- **Payment Disputes**: Disagreement over amount due
- **Scope Disputes**: What was/wasn't included
- **Damage Claims**: Property damage during service
- **Cancellation Disputes**: Who canceled and why

#### 7.1.2 Resolution Process
1. **Direct Negotiation** (Days 1-7):
   - Parties communicate directly to resolve
   - Platform messaging for record
   - Good faith effort required

2. **Platform Mediation** (Days 8-22):
   - Either party requests PopSystem mediation
   - Mediation fee: $150 (split between parties)
   - Evidence submission: Photos, messages, documentation
   - PopSystem makes non-binding recommendation
   - 15-day mediation period

3. **Binding Arbitration** (Day 23+):
   - If mediation fails, binding arbitration per main TOS
   - Arbitration costs per AAA rules
   - Decision is final and binding

#### 7.1.3 Evidence Requirements
- Contemporaneous documentation (photos, notes)
- Platform message history
- Bid/contract documents
- Change orders and approvals
- Completion photos and sign-offs
- Expert reports (if applicable)

### 7.2 Payment Hold During Disputes

#### 7.2.1 Automatic Hold
- Payment automatically held when dispute filed
- Funds remain in escrow until resolution
- Partial releases possible (undisputed portions)
- Interest does not accrue during hold

#### 7.2.2 Resolution Outcomes
- **Provider Wins**: Full payment released (minus platform fee)
- **Customer Wins**: Full or partial refund issued
- **Split Decision**: Payment divided per determination
- **Settlement**: Parties agree to resolution terms

### 7.3 Escalation to Legal Action

#### 7.3.1 Arbitration Agreement
- Binding arbitration required (per main TOS)
- AAA Commercial Arbitration Rules apply
- Location: Provider's state or customer's state (customer choice)
- Costs: Per AAA fee schedule
- Right to attorney (but not required)

#### 7.3.2 Exceptions
- Small claims court permitted (under jurisdictional limits)
- Emergency injunctive relief
- IP infringement claims
- Criminal matters

- Provider may dispute factually false or abusive reviews
- Evidence required (photos, messages, documents)
- PopSystem reviews dispute within 5 business days
- May remove, edit, or flag review
- Customer notified of dispute
- Decision final

### 15.3 Rating Impact

#### 15.3.1 Display and Ranking
- Overall rating displayed on provider profile
- Number of reviews shown
- Recent ratings weighted more heavily
- High-rated providers ranked higher in search
- Elite status at 4.8+ average

#### 15.3.2 Low Rating Consequences
- Below 3.5: Performance review triggered
- Below 3.0: Automatic probation
- Below 2.5: Suspension pending review
- Persistent low ratings: Termination
