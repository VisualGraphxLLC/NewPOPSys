# Partner Agreements Framework
**PopSystem Platform Partnership Models**

> **DISCLAIMER**: This is a strategic framework document for planning purposes only. This does NOT constitute legal advice and must be reviewed and finalized by qualified legal counsel before use with actual partners.

---

## Overview

This document outlines templates and key terms for various partnership arrangements to grow PopSystem's market reach and service capabilities.

**Partnership Types Covered**:
1. Referral Partner Agreement
2. Implementation Partner Agreement
3. White-Label Reseller Agreement
4. OEM Agreement

---

## 1. Referral Partner Agreement

### 1.1 Overview
**Purpose**: Engage consultants, industry experts, and complementary service providers to refer customers to PopSystem.

**Partner Types**:
- Print industry consultants
- Business coaches and advisors
- Equipment vendors (printers, finishing equipment)
- Industry associations
- Software vendors (complementary products)
- Marketing agencies serving print industry

### 1.2 Key Terms

#### Partner Obligations
- **Referral Activity**:
  - Identify potential customers
  - Introduce PopSystem to prospects
  - Provide basic information about platform
  - Submit referrals through partner portal

- **Compliance**:
  - Comply with applicable laws
  - No false or misleading representations
  - Use approved marketing materials only
  - Maintain professional reputation

- **Confidentiality**:
  - Protect PopSystem confidential information
  - Non-disclosure of pricing, roadmap, customer lists
  - Survival beyond agreement term

#### PopSystem Obligations
- **Support**:
  - Provide marketing materials
  - Partner training (product overview)
  - Access to partner portal
  - Referral tracking system
  - Regular communication and updates

- **Sales Process**:
  - Follow up on qualified referrals within 48 hours
  - Keep partner informed of referral status
  - Handle all sales, contracts, and billing
  - Provide product demonstrations

#### Compensation Structure
**Commission Tiers**:

**Tier 1: Standard Referral**
- Partner introduces prospect to PopSystem
- PopSystem handles all sales activity
- Commission: 10% of first year Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Payment: 30 days after customer's first payment
- Minimum: $100 per referral

**Tier 2: Assisted Referral**
- Partner participates in initial meeting
- Provides context and customer insights
- Commission: 15% of first year ARR
- Payment: 30 days after customer's first payment

**Tier 3: Qualified Opportunity**
- Partner pre-qualifies customer (budget, authority, need, timeline)
- Provides detailed opportunity brief
- Commission: 20% of first year ARR
- Payment: 30 days after customer's first payment
- Bonus: Additional 5% if customer upgrades to Enterprise within year 1

**Recurring Commissions**:
- Optional: 5% of ARR in years 2-3 (for active partners)
- Requires ongoing engagement and support
- Minimum 5 qualified referrals per year to qualify

#### Qualified Referral Criteria
A referral is "qualified" when:
- Contact information provided (name, company, email, phone)
- Basic qualifying information (company size, current situation)
- Prospect expects contact from PopSystem
- Not existing PopSystem customer or active opportunity
- Not on "do not contact" list

#### Attribution and Tracking
- **Attribution Period**: 90 days from referral submission
- **First Touch Attribution**: First referrer receives credit
- **Partner Portal**: Real-time tracking of referral status
- **Reporting**: Monthly statements with referral pipeline

#### Payment Terms
- **Schedule**: Net 30 from customer's first payment
- **Method**: ACH, wire transfer, or check
- **Minimum Payout**: $100 (otherwise carries forward)
- **Tax Forms**: W-9/W-8 required (1099 issued if applicable)
- **Currency**: USD

#### Marketing and Branding
**Partner May**:
- Describe relationship as "PopSystem Referral Partner"
- Use "Powered by PopSystem" badge (with approval)
- Link to PopSystem website
- Share approved case studies and materials

**Partner May NOT**:
- Use PopSystem logo without written approval
- Modify PopSystem marketing materials
- Create derivative branding
- Imply endorsement beyond scope
- Advertise on PopSystem trademarks (PPC, etc.)

#### Term and Termination
**Term**:
- Initial: 1 year from effective date
- Renewal: Automatic annual renewal
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice

**Termination for Cause**:
- Material breach (30-day cure period)
- Violation of confidentiality
- Misrepresentation or fraud
- Damage to PopSystem reputation
- Legal/regulatory violation

**Effect of Termination**:
- Cease referring customers immediately
- Stop using PopSystem branding
- Referrals in pipeline honored (if qualified before termination)
- Commissions owed still paid
- Confidentiality survives

#### Relationship
- **Independent Contractors**: Not employees, agents, or partners
- **No Authority**: Partner cannot bind PopSystem
- **No Exclusivity**: Either party may pursue other relationships
- **Territory**: Typically worldwide (unless restricted)

## 2. Implementation Partner Agreement

### 2.1 Overview
**Purpose**: Engage experienced consultants and agencies to provide implementation, training, and support services to PopSystem customers.

**Partner Types**:
- Print industry consultants
- Workflow optimization experts
- Change management consultants
- Training organizations
- System integrators
- IT service providers

### 2.2 Key Terms

#### Partner Obligations
- **Services Provided**:
  - PopSystem implementation and configuration
  - Workflow design and optimization
  - Data migration from legacy systems
  - Custom integrations (with APIs)
  - User training and change management
  - Ongoing support (optional)

- **Certification**:
  - Complete PopSystem Implementation Certification
  - Maintain at least 2 certified consultants on staff
  - Annual recertification
  - Minimum project experience requirements

- **Quality Standards**:
  - Follow PopSystem implementation best practices
  - Use approved methodologies
  - Maintain customer satisfaction (measured)
  - Minimum 4.0/5.0 customer rating average
  - Remediate issues at own expense

#### PopSystem Obligations
- **Enablement**:
  - Implementation certification program (40 hours)
  - Access to technical documentation
  - Dedicated partner support channel
  - Regular partner webinars and updates
  - Annual partner conference

- **Referrals**:
  - Refer customers seeking implementation help
  - List on partner directory (if certified)
  - Opportunity to bid on larger projects
  - Customer matching based on expertise

- **Support**:
  - Technical escalation path
  - Pre-sales technical support
  - Access to product roadmap
  - Beta access to new features

#### Compensation Structure
**Model**: Partner bills customer directly

**Suggested Rate Guidance**:
- Junior consultant: $100-150/hour
- Senior consultant: $150-250/hour
- Principal consultant: $250-350/hour
- Project-based pricing encouraged

**PopSystem Revenue Share**:
- **Option A: No Revenue Share**
  - Partner keeps 100% of services revenue
  - No payment to PopSystem
  - Partner acquires customers independently

- **Option B: PopSystem-Sourced Projects**
  - If PopSystem refers customer to partner
  - Partner pays 20% of project value to PopSystem
  - Payment: Net 30 from partner's receipt of customer payment
  - Ensures quality partners prioritized for referrals

**Volume Incentives**:
- Tier 1 (0-5 projects/year): Standard terms
- Tier 2 (6-10 projects/year): 10% discount on PopSystem licenses for partner's customers
- Tier 3 (11+ projects/year): 15% discount + co-marketing opportunities

#### Certification Requirements
**Initial Certification**:
- 40-hour training program (online + virtual instructor-led)
- Hands-on lab exercises
- Certification exam (80% passing score)
- Shadow certified partner on 1 implementation
- Complete 1 supervised implementation
- Cost: $2,500 per consultant

**Recertification**:
- Annual requirement
- 8 hours continuing education
- Exam on new features
- Maintain performance standards
- Cost: $500 per consultant

**Firm Requirements**:
- Minimum 2 certified consultants
- Maintain professional liability insurance ($1M+)
- Business license and good standing
- Background checks on consultants
- Signed implementation partner agreement

#### Customer Relationship
**Clearly Defined Roles**:
- PopSystem: Product vendor, platform support, account management
- Partner: Implementation services, training, workflow design
- Customer: Clear understanding of roles

**Engagement Model**:
- Partner contracts directly with customer for services
- PopSystem contracts with customer for software
- Separate statements of work
- Partner responsible for service delivery
- PopSystem not liable for partner services

#### Quality Assurance
**Monitoring**:
- Customer satisfaction surveys (post-implementation)
- Implementation review (spot checks)
- Compliance audits
- Customer complaint tracking

**Performance Standards**:
- Average customer rating: 4.0/5.0 minimum
- On-time delivery: 80% minimum
- Budget adherence: Within 10% of estimate
- Certification current: 100% of consultants

**Remediation**:
- Below-standard performance: Improvement plan
- Continued issues: Suspend new referrals
- Serious issues: Termination
- Customer harm: Partner responsible for make-good

#### Intellectual Property
**Partner Work Product**:
- Partner owns custom work product created for customers
- Customer receives license to use
- PopSystem may request copy for reference
- No reuse of customer confidential information

**PopSystem IP**:
- Partner receives license to use PopSystem materials for implementations
- No reverse engineering
- No derivative products
- License terminates with agreement

**Joint Developments**:
- Reusable accelerators, templates, tools
- Jointly owned or license granted to PopSystem
- PopSystem may share with other partners
- Partner credited

#### Marketing and Branding
**Partner Listing**:
- Listed on PopSystem partner directory
- Profile with expertise areas
- Customer reviews displayed
- Logo and description

**Co-Marketing**:
- Joint case studies (with customer approval)
- Partner testimonials
- Co-branded webinars (for top partners)
- Joint conference presence (selected partners)

**Partner Badge**:
- "PopSystem Certified Implementation Partner"
- Tiered badging (Silver, Gold, Platinum based on performance)
- Use in marketing materials
- Guidelines provided

#### Term and Termination
**Term**:
- Initial: 2 years
- Renewal: Automatic (unless terminated)

**Termination**:
- Either party: 90 days written notice
- For cause: Immediate (breach, quality issues, fraud)
- Loss of certification: 30 days to remedy or terminate
- Mutual agreement: Anytime

**Wind-Down**:
- Complete active projects
- No new projects accepted
- Remove from partner directory
- Return confidential materials

## 3. White-Label Reseller Agreement

### 3.1 Overview
**Purpose**: Enable partners to resell PopSystem platform under their own brand to specific market segments or geographies.

**Partner Types**:
- Print equipment manufacturers
- Industry-specific software vendors
- Regional VARs (Value-Added Resellers)
- Franchise organizations
- Industry associations

### 3.2 Key Terms

#### Grant of Rights
**License to Partner**:
- Non-exclusive right to resell PopSystem
- Specific territory or vertical (if exclusive)
- Use partner branding on customer-facing elements
- Partner sets pricing (within guidelines)
- Partner owns customer relationship

**PopSystem Reserved Rights**:
- Own platform and underlying IP
- Provide platform updates and maintenance
- Set minimum feature requirements
- Approve major customizations
- Sell direct in territory (if non-exclusive)

#### White-Label Capabilities
**Partner Branding**:
- Custom domain name (e.g., partner.com)
- Partner logo and color scheme
- Custom login page
- Partner name throughout UI
- Partner support contact information

**PopSystem Branding**:
- "Powered by PopSystem" footer (may be minimized)
- PopSystem mentioned in Terms of Service
- PopSystem retains platform rights

**Customization Limits**:
- UI branding: Extensive
- Feature configuration: Within platform capabilities
- Custom development: Requires separate agreement
- Integration: Partner responsible

#### Pricing and Revenue Share
**Wholesale Pricing Model**:

**Tier-Based Discounts**:
- Tier 1 (1-50 customers): 30% discount off list price
- Tier 2 (51-100 customers): 35% discount off list price
- Tier 3 (101-250 customers): 40% discount off list price
- Tier 4 (251+ customers): 45% discount off list price

**Calculation**:
- Partner pays PopSystem wholesale price
- Partner sets retail price (suggested: list price + 10-30%)
- Partner retains margin
- Example: List price $199/mo, wholesale $139/mo (Tier 2), partner sells at $249/mo, keeps $110/mo margin

**Minimum Commitments**:
- Year 1: $50,000 ARR minimum
- Year 2: $100,000 ARR minimum
- Year 3: $200,000 ARR minimum
- Or risk losing white-label status

**Payment Terms**:
- Monthly billing based on active customers
- Net 30 payment terms
- Annual prepay option: Additional 10% discount
- Quarterly true-up reconciliation

#### Service Levels
**PopSystem Provides**:
- Platform hosting and infrastructure
- 99.5% uptime SLA
- Platform updates and new features
- Security and compliance
- Core technical support (escalations)
- API access

**Partner Provides**:
- Customer onboarding and training
- First-level customer support
- Account management
- Customization and configuration
- Implementation services
- Billing and collections

#### Support Model
**Tier 1 Support (Partner)**:
- Customer-facing support
- Handle all customer inquiries
- Troubleshoot common issues
- Configuration assistance
- Partner responsible for staffing and training

**Tier 2 Support (PopSystem)**:
- Technical escalations from partner
- Platform bugs and issues
- Feature requests
- Integration support
- Response time: 4 hours (business hours)

**Support Tools Provided**:
- Partner support portal
- Knowledge base access
- Partner training program
- Regular partner office hours
- Dedicated partner success manager (Tier 3+)

#### Marketing and Lead Generation
**Partner Responsibilities**:
- All marketing to target customers
- Lead generation
- Sales process and closing
- Marketing materials creation
- Event participation
- Partner funds marketing

**PopSystem Provides**:
- White-label marketing templates
- Case study support
- Product messaging guidance
- Demo environment
- Approval of marketing claims

**Co-Marketing** (for larger partners):
- Joint webinars
- Co-branded content
- Event sponsorship sharing
- Lead sharing programs

#### Territory and Exclusivity
**Non-Exclusive (Standard)**:
- Partner may sell anywhere
- PopSystem may sell anywhere
- Other partners in same territory
- No exclusivity rights

**Exclusive Territory** (for qualified partners):
- Requirements:
  - Minimum $500,000 ARR commitment
  - Exclusive for defined geography or vertical
  - Quarterly minimum targets
  - Marketing investment commitment
- Benefits:
  - PopSystem refers all leads in territory
  - No competing partners in territory
  - 5% additional discount
- Risk: Lose exclusivity if minimums not met

#### Customer Data and Ownership
**Customer Relationship**:
- Partner owns customer relationship
- Partner's terms of service (incorporating PopSystem terms)
- Partner handles billing and collections
- Customer data subject to DPA

**Data Access**:
- Partner: Full access to their customers' data
- PopSystem: Access for platform operation and support
- Customer: Can export data anytime

**Portability**:
- If agreement terminates, customers may:
  - Move to PopSystem direct (with notice)
  - Move to another partner
  - Export data and leave
- Transition support provided (90 days)

#### Customization and Development
**Configuration**:
- Partner may configure within platform capabilities
- Templates, workflows, branding
- No custom code required
- No additional fees

**Custom Development**:
- Requires separate development agreement
- Partner pays development costs
- Ownership negotiated per project
- PopSystem approval required

**Integration**:
- Partner may build integrations via API
- Partner owns integration code
- Must meet security standards
- PopSystem may list in marketplace

#### Term and Termination
**Term**:
- Initial: 3 years
- Renewal: 3-year terms (mutual agreement)
- Long-term partnership model

**Termination**:
- Mutual agreement: Anytime
- For convenience: 180 days notice
- For cause: 30 days (with cure period)
- Failure to meet minimums: 90 days notice

**Customer Transition**:
- 90-day transition period
- Customers offered direct PopSystem contract
- Or transfer to another partner
- Data portability ensured
- No disruption to service

## 4. OEM Agreement

### 4.1 Overview
**Purpose**: Enable equipment manufacturers and technology vendors to embed PopSystem capabilities into their products.

**Partner Types**:
- Print equipment manufacturers (digital presses, wide format, etc.)
- Workflow software vendors
- MIS/ERP system providers
- Hardware vendors (cutters, finishers, etc.)
- Automation technology providers

### 4.2 Key Terms

#### Grant of Rights
**License to OEM Partner**:
- Embed PopSystem functionality in partner product
- Rebrand as partner's own (deep white-label)
- Distribute to partner's customers
- Bundle with partner hardware/software
- Sub-license to end customers

**Integration Models**:
- **Embedded**: PopSystem runs within partner product
- **Integrated**: Deep bi-directional integration via API
- **Bundled**: Separate but sold together
- **Powered By**: Partner product powered by PopSystem backend

#### Technical Integration
**Integration Depth**:
- Single sign-on (SSO)
- Shared data models
- Unified user interface
- API integration
- Webhook notifications
- Embedded components

**Development Requirements**:
- Joint development plan
- Technical specifications
- Testing and certification
- Ongoing compatibility

**Support Model**:
- Partner: Primary support for their product
- PopSystem: Support for embedded components
- Joint escalation process
- Shared troubleshooting

#### Pricing and Revenue
**OEM Pricing Models**:

**Model A: Per-Unit Licensing**:
- Partner pays per installation/customer
- Tiered pricing based on volume
- Example:
  - 1-100 units: $50/month per unit
  - 101-500 units: $40/month per unit
  - 501+ units: $30/month per unit
- Partner adds markup

**Model B: Revenue Share**:
- Partner pays percentage of revenue from customers
- Typical: 25-35% of partner's product revenue
- Quarterly true-up
- Minimum quarterly payment
- Transparency required

**Model C: Flat Fee + Revenue Share**:
- Annual platform fee: $100,000 - $500,000
- Plus: 10-15% revenue share
- Provides base revenue to PopSystem
- Aligns incentives for growth

**Hardware Bundle**:
- Special pricing for hardware bundles
- Lifetime license option (for hardware sale)
- One-time fee: $3,000 - $5,000 per unit
- Ongoing support: $50/month per unit

#### Minimum Commitments
**Annual Minimums**:
- Year 1: $200,000
- Year 2: $400,000
- Year 3: $750,000
- Or negotiate based on market size

**Volume Commitments**:
- Minimum installations per year
- Marketing investment requirements
- Sales team training
- Trade show presence

#### Intellectual Property
**PopSystem IP**:
- PopSystem retains all platform IP
- Partner receives license to embed
- No transfer of ownership
- No reverse engineering

**Partner IP**:
- Partner retains product IP
- PopSystem receives no rights to partner product
- Joint IP governed separately

**Joint Developments**:
- Ownership negotiated per project
- Typically: PopSystem owns platform enhancements
- Partner owns product-specific features
- Cross-license if integrated

**Trademarks**:
- Partner may use PopSystem marks per guidelines
- "Powered by PopSystem" permitted
- PopSystem may use partner marks for reference
- No implied endorsement

#### Marketing and Go-to-Market
**Partner Obligations**:
- Active marketing of bundled solution
- Sales team training on PopSystem
- Annual marketing commitment ($ or %)
- Include in product roadmap
- Trade show participation

**PopSystem Support**:
- Co-branded marketing materials
- Joint press releases
- Case study development
- Sales training and enablement
- Trade show support
- Lead sharing

**Exclusivity Considerations**:
- Partner may request category exclusivity
- Example: Exclusive digital press manufacturer
- Requires significant commitments
- Higher revenue guarantees
- Extended term (5+ years)

#### Service Levels and Support
**PopSystem SLA**:
- 99.9% uptime for OEM integrations
- Priority support for OEM partners
- Dedicated technical account manager
- Advance notice of changes
- Beta access to new features

**Partner Obligations**:
- Maintain integration compatibility
- Test with new releases
- Support their customers
- Escalate platform issues promptly

**Upgrade Coordination**:
- Advance notice: 90 days for major changes
- Testing window provided
- Coordinated rollout
- Backwards compatibility (where possible)

#### Term and Termination
**Term**:
- Initial: 5 years (longer-term partnership)
- Renewal: 5-year terms
- Significant investment justifies longer term

**Termination**:
- Mutual agreement: Anytime
- For convenience: 365 days notice (long wind-down)
- For cause: Material breach, 90 days cure
- Failure to meet minimums: 180 days notice

**Wind-Down**:
- Continued support for existing customers (12 months)
- Migration path for customers
- Data portability
- Source code escrow (if negotiated)

#### Customer Transition Rights
**If Agreement Terminates**:
- End customers may:
  - Continue with partner's product (if feasible)
  - Migrate to PopSystem direct
  - Export data
- Transition support: 12 months
- No forced churn

## General Terms Across All Partner Types

### Confidentiality
- Mutual NDA
- Protect confidential information
- 5-year survival
- Standard exceptions (public domain, etc.)
- Equitable relief available

### Representations and Warranties
**Each Party Represents**:
- Authority to enter agreement
- Compliance with laws
- No infringement
- Proper business licenses
- No conflicts of interest

**PopSystem Warrants**:
- Platform performs as documented
- Security measures implemented
- Ownership of IP
- Disclaimer: No other warranties (as-is for most aspects)

### Liability and Indemnification
**Liability Cap**:
- Each party: Limited to fees paid/received in prior 12 months
- Minimum: $100,000
- Exceptions: Confidentiality, IP infringement, gross negligence

**Indemnification**:
- Each party indemnifies for:
  - IP infringement
  - Breach of agreement
  - Negligence or willful misconduct
  - Violation of law

### Insurance Requirements
**Required Coverage**:
- General liability: $2,000,000
- Professional liability: $2,000,000 (for implementation partners)
- Cyber liability: $1,000,000 (for resellers and OEMs)
- Workers' compensation: As required by law

**Proof**:
- Certificates of insurance annually
- PopSystem named as additional insured
- 30 days notice of cancellation

### Compliance
**Legal Compliance**:
- All applicable laws and regulations
- Data protection (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Export controls
- Anti-corruption laws
- Industry-specific regulations

**Ethics**:
- No bribery or kickbacks
- Arm's-length transactions
- Disclosure of conflicts
- Compliance hotline

### Dispute Resolution
**Escalation**:
- Working level resolution
- Executive escalation
- Good faith negotiation (30 days)

**Mediation**:
- Non-binding mediation before litigation
- AAA commercial mediation
- Costs shared equally

**Arbitration/Litigation**:
- May be required (or optional) per agreement
- Governing law: [Specify state/country]
- Venue: [Specify location]
- Prevailing party attorney fees
