# Financial Model

## Executive Summary

This document outlines PopSystem's 5-year financial model framework, including revenue projections, cost structure, unit economics, and scenario analysis. The model demonstrates a path to $50M+ ARR with strong unit economics, capital efficiency, and a clear path to profitability by Year 4.

**Key Highlights**:
- 5-year ARR growth: $500K → $50M (191% CAGR)
- Gross margin target: 75% at scale
- Path to profitability: Year 4
- LTV:CAC ratio: 17:1 at scale
- Capital required: $15M-$20M total through Series A

---

## Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams

PopSystem operates a dual revenue model combining predictable SaaS subscription revenue with high-margin transaction processing fees.

#### 1. SaaS Subscription Revenue (60% at scale)

**Pricing Structure**:

| Tier | Monthly Base | Per Student/Month | Target Segment | Students Range |
|------|--------------|-------------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Starter** | $99 | $0.50 | Small schools | 0-500 |
| **Professional** | $299 | $1.00 | Medium schools, multi-facility | 500-2,000 |
| **Enterprise** | $999 | $1.50 | Large districts | 2,000+ |

**Average Contract Values**:
- Starter tier (250 avg students): $224/month = $2,688/year
- Professional tier (1,000 avg students): $1,299/month = $15,588/year
- Enterprise tier (5,000 avg students): $8,499/month = $101,988/year

**Blended ARPU**: $1.50 per student per month ($18/year per student)

#### 2. Transaction Processing Revenue (40% at scale)

**Fee Structure**:
- Credit/Debit cards: 2.75% + $0.30 per transaction
- ACH/eCheck: 1.0% + $0.25 per transaction
- Alternative payments: Variable (1.5%-3.5%)

**Transaction Volume Assumptions**:
- 60% of schools use PopSystem for payment processing (opt-in)
- Average payment volume: $200 per student per year
- 70% credit card, 25% ACH, 5% other
- Blended take rate: 2.4%

**Transaction Revenue per Student**: $200 × 2.4% = $4.80/year

**Total Blended Revenue per Student**: $18 (SaaS) + $4.80 (transactions) = $22.80/year

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Calculation**: Total S&M Spend / New Customers Acquired

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 | Year 4 | Year 5 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **S&M Spend** | $252K | $1.13M | $3.92M | $9.28M | $17.1M |
| **New Customers** | 100 | 500 | 2,000 | 5,000 | 10,000 |
| **CAC** | $2,520 | $2,260 | $1,960 | $1,856 | $1,710 |

**CAC Trend**: Decreases over time due to:
- Brand recognition and word-of-mouth
- Partner channel development (lower cost)
- Marketing efficiency and automation
- Product-led growth for smaller schools
- District expansion (land-and-expand)

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Calculation**: (Avg Annual Revenue × Gross Margin) × Avg Customer Lifetime

**Base Case Assumptions**:
- Average annual revenue per customer: $10,800 (at scale)
- Gross margin: 75%
- Average customer lifetime: 7 years (schools rarely churn)
- Expansion revenue: 20% over lifetime

**LTV Calculation**:
- Annual gross profit: $10,800 × 75% = $8,100
- Base lifetime value: $8,100 × 7 = $56,700
- With expansion: $56,700 × 1.20 = $68,040

**Conservative LTV** (5-year, no expansion): $40,500
**Base Case LTV** (7-year, 20% expansion): $68,040
**Optimistic LTV** (10-year, 30% expansion): $105,300

### LTV:CAC Ratio

| Scenario | LTV | CAC (Year 3) | LTV:CAC Ratio |
|----------|-----|--------------|---------------|
| **Conservative** | $40,500 | $2,000 | 20:1 |
| **Base Case** | $68,040 | $2,000 | 34:1 |
| **Optimistic** | $105,300 | $2,000 | 53:1 |

**Target**: >3:1 is venture-scale; 34:1 is exceptional

### Payback Period

**Calculation**: CAC / (Monthly Gross Profit per Customer)

**At Scale**:
- CAC: $2,000
- Monthly revenue per customer: $900
- Gross margin: 75%
- Monthly gross profit: $675
- **Payback Period**: 2.96 months (~3 months)

**By Year**:
- Year 1: 8 months (higher CAC, lower revenue)
- Year 2: 6 months
- Year 3: 4 months
- Year 4+: 3 months

**Target**: <12 months; <6 months is excellent

### Magic Number

**Calculation**: (Net New ARR in Quarter) / (S&M Spend in Prior Quarter)

**Interpretation**:
- <0.5: Poor, unprofitable growth
- 0.5-0.75: Acceptable
- 0.75-1.0: Good
- >1.0: Excellent, efficient growth

**PopSystem Targets**:
- Year 1: 0.8 (proving model)
- Year 2: 1.2 (efficiency improving)
- Year 3+: 1.5-2.0 (highly efficient growth)

## Conclusion

PopSystem's financial model demonstrates:
