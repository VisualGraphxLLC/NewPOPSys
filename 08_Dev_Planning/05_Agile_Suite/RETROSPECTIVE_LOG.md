## Retrospective Formats

### Standard Format (Most Common)
- What went well?
- What to improve?
- Action items

### Start/Stop/Continue
- **Start:** What should we begin doing?
- **Stop:** What should we stop doing?
- **Continue:** What should we keep doing?

### 4 L's
- **Liked:** What did we like?
- **Learned:** What did we learn?
- **Lacked:** What did we lack?
- **Longed for:** What did we wish for?

### Mad/Sad/Glad
- **Mad:** What frustrated us?
- **Sad:** What disappointed us?
- **Glad:** What made us happy?

### Sailboat
- **Wind (helping us):** What's pushing us forward?
- **Anchor (holding us back):** What's slowing us down?
- **Rocks (risks):** What risks are ahead?
- **Island (goal):** Where are we heading?

## Best Practices

### For Facilitators
1. **Rotate facilitation** - Different perspectives each sprint
2. **Create safe space** - Encourage honest feedback without blame
3. **Timebox discussions** - Keep focused and productive
4. **Focus on actions** - Every improvement should have an owner
5. **Follow up** - Review previous action items at the start

### For Participants
1. **Come prepared** - Think about the sprint beforehand
2. **Be specific** - Avoid vague comments
3. **Stay constructive** - Focus on improvement, not complaints
4. **Volunteer for actions** - Take ownership of improvements
5. **Be honest** - Speak up about issues
