**PrecisionPOP**

Product Walkthrough

*UX Evaluation Guide for v2 Redesign Planning*

Test Account: Good 2 Go Store #119

Idaho Falls, ID

December 2024

**1. Introduction**

This document provides a guided walkthrough of the PrecisionPOP platform from the perspective of a store manager at Good 2 Go Store #119 in Idaho Falls, Idaho. The walkthrough is designed to evaluate the current user experience and identify opportunities for improvement in the v2 redesign.

Each section explores a different area of the platform, with evaluation questions highlighted in red italics to guide the UX assessment process.

**Test Account Details**

Store: Good 2 Go #119

Location: 250 S 45th East, Idaho Falls, ID 83401

Phone: (208)-535-5931

**2. First Impressions: The Store Dashboard**

Upon logging in, users land on the Store Profile page with the Exterior tab active. This page establishes the store\'s identity within the system and provides access to key information panels.

![Dashboard - Exterior Tab showing store attributes and Kits panel](media/93e8a5e20806cffce167d7f91dbb16d36d78d231.jpg "Dashboard - Exterior Tab showing store attributes and Kits panel"){width="5.729166666666667in" height="4.166666666666667in"}

*Dashboard - Exterior Tab showing store attributes and Kits panel*

**What We See**

The dashboard displays the store name, number (#119), address, and phone number. A Good 2 Go logo appears in the right panel. The Kits & Inventory sidebar shows 25 promotional kits with color-coded status badges.

**Evaluation Questions**

> *• Does the layout effectively communicate the store\'s identity?*
>
> *• Is the Kits & Inventory sidebar discoverable and scannable?*
>
> *• What actions might a store manager want to take from this view?*

**3. Understanding Your Store: The Interior Tab**

Clicking the Interior tab reveals the store survey system---over 50 questions capturing physical store attributes that drive promotional planning decisions.

![Dashboard - Interior Tab showing 50+ survey questions](media/c82fbb2f5bf8cec991896861126b6bb124cb7b90.jpg "Dashboard - Interior Tab showing 50+ survey questions"){width="5.729166666666667in" height="4.166666666666667in"}

*Dashboard - Interior Tab showing 50+ survey questions*

**Survey Categories**

Questions cover: Store type and environment, Equipment presence (coolers, dispensers, ATMs), Service offerings (car wash, food service), Display capabilities and traffic patterns.

**Evaluation Questions**

> *• Is the survey format approachable or overwhelming?*
>
> *• How might users understand the purpose of each question?*
>
> *• What feedback mechanisms exist for incorrect or outdated data?*

**11. Walkthrough Summary**

This walkthrough has explored the complete PrecisionPOP experience from the perspective of a Good 2 Go store manager. Key areas for v2 redesign consideration include:

**Information Architecture**

Navigation between Store Profile, Activity, Kit Reorders, and Store Photos tabs. Relationship between the main content area and Kits & Inventory sidebar. Discoverability of help resources and account settings.

**Core Workflows**

Kit receipt and installation status updates. Missing/damaged item reorder process. Store attribute survey completion. Photo documentation and management.

**Data Visualization**

Kit status badges and color coding. Activity log pagination and filtering. Large inventory list management (61+ items).

This document serves as a foundation for UX discussions and should be referenced alongside the detailed audit markdown files for each section.
