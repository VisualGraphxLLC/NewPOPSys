## UX Observations

### Strengths
1. **Professional branding** - Vivid Impact logo establishes credibility
2. **Clear video title** - Users know what they'll learn
3. **Standard video controls** - Familiar playback interface
4. **Short duration** - 1:37 is digestible for busy store managers
5. **Related content** - Suggested videos provide learning path
6. **Accessible from anywhere** - How To menu in main navigation

### Areas for Improvement
1. **Limited video library** - Only 2 videos total in How To menu
2. **No video transcript** - Should provide text alternative for accessibility
3. **No playback speed control** - Users can't speed up/slow down
4. **No chapter markers** - Can't jump to specific sections
5. **No search within videos** - Can't find specific topics
6. **No video description** - Missing summary of what's covered
7. **No related documentation** - Should link to written guides
8. **Single suggested video** - Could show more related content

### Suggested Video Topics (Missing)

### Accessibility Notes
- Video needs closed captions/subtitles
- Transcript should be available below video
- Video player needs keyboard controls
- Audio description track for visually impaired users
