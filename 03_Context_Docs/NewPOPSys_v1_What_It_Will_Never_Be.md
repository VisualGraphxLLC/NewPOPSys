This document exists to permanently shut down scope creep. If a request appears below, it is explicitly not part of v1, regardless of how reasonable it sounds.

# v1 Will Never Be an MIS

- No job costing or accounting

- No production scheduling

- No payroll or financial reporting

- No ERP replacement behavior

# v1 Will Never Be a Marketplace

- No installer bidding or matchmaking

- No open vendor marketplace

- No rating or review systems

- No dynamic pricing engines

# v1 Will Never Be Analytics-Heavy

- No benchmarking across brands

- No predictive analytics dashboards

- No AI-driven insights surfaced to users

- Data is collected, not mined (yet)

# v1 Will Never Store Data Forever

- No long-term archival storage

- No historical data warehouse

- 90-day retention after completion is fixed

# v1 Will Never Optimize for Everyone

- PSP adoption wins prioritization

- Brand UX matters only insofar as it helps PSPs win

- Store UX is execution-focused, not customizable

- Nice-to-haves lose to delivery discipline

# v1 Will Never Expand Scope Quietly

- All scope changes require tradeoffs

- Anything new pushes something else out

- Timeline and budget are fixed constraints

Rule of thumb: if it does not directly support campaign orchestration, PSP fulfillment efficiency, or store execution clarity, it is not v1.
