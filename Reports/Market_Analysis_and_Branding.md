### Market Gaps & Opportunities

#### 1. Unified POP Campaign Platform (PRIMARY OPPORTUNITY)

**Gap**: No market leader offers complete lifecycle:
```
Creative Design → Print Management → Fulfillment → Field Installation → Compliance Verification → ROI Tracking
```

**Current state**: Fragmented between creative agencies, print vendors, and retail execution platforms.

#### 2. SMB Market (11% penetration)

- 40% of small retailers untapped
- Barriers: High costs, complex enterprise solutions
- Opportunity: $25-50/month simplified solutions

#### 3. Print/Signage Integration

- Most retail execution platforms don't integrate with print fulfillment
- Separate systems (GearBox, ShopVOX) vs. execution platforms
- Opportunity: Unified ordering → tracking → verification

#### 4. Mid-Market Pricing Gap

- Only 28% of market
- Need: $30-75/user/month with enterprise features

#### 5. Emerging Markets

- Asia-Pacific fastest growing
- Requirements: Offline-first, low-bandwidth optimization

## Part 3: Branding & Design Guidelines

### Recommended Color Palette: Modern Teal/Cyan

**Why**: Differentiates from "generic blue SaaS" while maintaining trust. Combines blue's reliability with green's growth associations.

```
PRIMARY PALETTE
├── Primary:        #00ABB9 (Teal)
├── Primary Dark:   #012D3A (Navy)
├── Accent:         #FCD732 (Warm Yellow)
├── Success:        #00D97E (Green)
├── Warning:        #FFB020 (Amber)
├── Error:          #F45C3A (Coral Red)
└── Neutrals
    ├── 900:        #0F172A (Dark backgrounds)
    ├── 500:        #64748B (Secondary text)
    └── 100:        #F1F5F9 (Light backgrounds)
```

### Alternative Palettes

**Option 2: Navy + Emerald** (Enterprise-grade)
- Primary: `#1E40AF` (Deep Blue)
- Accent: `#10B981` (Emerald)

**Option 3: Purple + Citrus** (Maximum differentiation)
- Primary: `#7C3AED` (Violet)
- Accent: `#F59E0B` (Orange)

**Option 4: Minimalist Monochrome** (Data-heavy focus)
- Primary: `#1E293B` (Slate)
- Accent: `#14B8A6` (Teal highlights only)

### Typography

**Recommended: Inter (all weights)**

| Use | Weight | Size |
|-----|--------|------|
| H1 | Bold (700) | 32-36px |
| H2 | SemiBold (600) | 24-28px |
| H3 | SemiBold (600) | 18-20px |
| Body | Regular (400) | 14-16px |
| Caption | Regular (400) | 12-14px |

**Alternatives**:
- Plus Jakarta Sans (friendly, geometric)
- Geist (developer-focused, Swiss precision)

**Accessibility**:
- Minimum 14px body text
- Line height 1.5 for body
- 50-75 characters per line

---

### Iconography

**Style**: Outlined (stroke-based) for most uses, filled for active states

**Recommended Library**: Phosphor Icons
- 9,000+ icons
- Multiple styles (Regular, Thin, Bold, Fill, Duotone)
- MIT license (free)

**Alternatives**:
- Heroicons (minimalist, Tailwind team)
- Lucide (lightweight, framework-agnostic)

**Custom Icons Needed**:
- Shelf/planogram
- POP display materials
- Store visit/check-in
- Photo verification
- Compliance score
- Route/territory

---

### Layout Patterns

**Dashboard**: Card-based modular design
- 16-24px gaps between cards
- 20-32px internal padding
- 8-12px corner radius
- Subtle shadows for elevation

**Navigation**: Sidebar (desktop) + Bottom nav (mobile)
- Sidebar: 240-280px expanded, 64-80px collapsed
- Mobile: 3-5 primary actions in bottom nav
- FAB for primary action (Check In, Start Visit)

**Mobile-First for Field Workers**:
- Large touch targets (44x44px minimum)
- Offline-first data capture
- Photo-centric workflows
- Swipe gestures for task completion

---

### Micro-Interactions

**Timing**: 200-500ms, 60 FPS

**Loading States**:
- Skeleton screens (preferred over spinners)
- Progress bars for >2 second operations

**Feedback**:
- Toast notifications (top-right or bottom-center)
- 3-5 second auto-dismiss
- Semantic colors (green success, red error)

**Animations**:
- Button press: scale 0.95, 100ms
- Card hover: shadow lift, 200ms
- Modal: fade backdrop + scale content, 250ms
